Expanded details for legal and compliance review

Core Design Principles
  • Pay decoupled from headcount: Service fee stays the same whether the partner serves 10 or 100 users
  • Bonuses use ratios, not absolute numbers: e.g. "60% of clubs healthy" not "X clubs healthy"
  • Monthly cap on all types: No unlimited growth incentive
  • Every payment tied to verifiable deliverables: Reports, meeting logs, content, mentorship records
  • Contract type: Consulting / Content Service Agreement -- not "agent" or "distributor"
Red Line Triggers (auto compliance review)
  • Any partner hitting 90%+ of cap for 2 consecutive months
  • Any request to modify payment formula
  • Any new metric containing "player count" / "user count" / "registration count"
  • Any proposal linking one partner's income to another partner's performance
