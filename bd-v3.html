合格社区合作方定义（21天验证）

什么是"合格社区合作方"（Qualified Community Partner）

第一级

基础激活

Day 1 - 5
  • 完成注册并创建俱乐部
  • 首次独立组局，至少4名真实玩家
状态变为：已激活
第二级

运营验证

Day 6 - 14
  • 9天内独立组局5场以上
  • 牌局完成率70%以上
  • 活跃天数5天以上
  • 至少3名回访玩家
状态变为：已验证
第三级

社区质量验证

Day 15 - 21
  • 持续组局3场以上
  • 俱乐部内有10名以上有效活跃用户
  • 有效活跃用户标准：每周手牌数 > 50
状态变为：合格社区合作方 → 触发佣金结算
⚠ 重要说明

有效活跃用户定义：每周手牌数 > 50。这是硬性标准，不是"注册了就算"。一个合格社区合作方 = 一个能持续运转、有10+真实活跃玩家的扑克社区。

Danny英文摘要

可折叠面板 · 完整英文提案摘要

BD Agreement (Community Development)

  • Base salary: $0/month (Phase 0 trial)
  • Commission: $200 per Qualified Community Partner (QCP)
  • No monthly cap on commissions
  • Settlement: Monthly, paid by the 5th of next month
  • Clawback: 50% if community fully inactive within 60 days

QCP Definition (21-Day 3-Tier Verification)

  • Tier 1 (Day 1-5): Register, create club, first game with 4+ real players
  • Tier 2 (Day 6-14): 5+ games in 9 days, 70%+ completion rate, 3+ returning players
  • Tier 3 (Day 15-21): 3+ games, 10+ active users (50+ hands/week each)
  • Active user definition: 50+ hands per week (hard requirement)

Community Growth Bonuses (per community, one-time)

  • 20+ active users: +$150
  • 40+ active users: +$300
  • 3 months sustained 20+ active users: +$200 (stability bonus)
  • Quarterly top BD award: +$300 (when multiple BDs)

Content Agreement (Optional, Separate Contract)

  • Base: $200/month
  • Deliverables: 4 short videos + 2 social media posts per month
  • Quality bonus: +$100 if all pass review
  • Monthly cap: $300

Phase Progression

  • Phase 0 (0-3mo): $0 base + $200/QCP. Unlock Phase 1 with 3 QCPs + 50% survival
  • Phase 1 (3-6mo): $300/mo base + $250/QCP + regional exclusive rights
  • Phase 2 (6mo+): $500/mo base + $300/QCP + event co-sponsorship
